Key Features

Timetable Management

View your daily and weekly schedule, including class timing, location, and subject details. Get notified about upcoming classes.

Attendance Tracking

Monitor your attendance for each subject, view history, and get alerts when attendance drops below the required threshold.

CGPA & Grades

Access your semester-wise CGPA, individual subject marks, and track your academic progress throughout your degree.

Friend Connections

Find and connect with other students, send friend requests, and build your own university network.

Placement Drives

Stay updated with the latest campus placement drives, eligibility criteria, and registration deadlines.

University Messages

Access all your university messages and communications in one organized dashboard.
